# ADITYA CONSTRUCTION - ENTERPRISE WEBSITE & MULTI-PORTAL MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

## PROJECT OBJECTIVE

Upgrade and extend the existing Aditya Construction website into a fully integrated enterprise management ecosystem consisting of:

1. Public Website
2. Operations Portal
3. Owner / Authorizer Portal
4. Staff Portal
5. Clientele Portal

The system must be scalable, role-based, database-driven, and fully manageable through administrative dashboards.
